Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you use KISAN-SATHI, we may collect information you provide directly, such as your name, phone number, email address, district/village, and farming details (soil type, crop preferences, season). We also collect location data when you use the "Use My GPS Location" feature, and basic usage data to improve the service.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide personalized crop, weather, and market price recommendations
  • To send weather alerts and farming advisories relevant to your location
  • To respond to support requests submitted through the Help & Support section
  • To improve the accuracy and usefulness of KISAN-SATHI's features over time

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share limited data with government scheme portals only when you choose to click through to "Apply/Details" links, at which point that third-party site's own privacy policy applies.

4. Data Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Your Choices

You can update or correct your profile information at any time from the "Edit profile" option in your account menu, and you can disable SMS weather alerts from Settings. To request deletion of your data, contact us using the details below.

6. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, reach out via the Help & Support section, email support@kisansathi.app, or call our helpline at 1800-123-4567.

This policy may be updated from time to time. Significant changes will be communicated through the app.
