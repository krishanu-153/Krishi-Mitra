Agricultural Advisories and Schemes

Latest Alerts & Tips

Weather Alert

Pre-Monsoon Irrigation Advisory

Heavy to moderate rainfall is expected in Southern Bengal over the next 48 hours. Farmers are advised to postpone irrigation and fertilizer application to prevent nutrient runoff.

Pest Warning

Fall Armyworm Detection

Cases of Fall Armyworm detected in Maize crops. Monitor crops closely. If detected, spray Emamectin Benzoate 5% SG (0.4g/liter) or Spinetoram 11.7% SC (0.5ml/liter) in clear weather.

Govt Advisory

Kharif Seed Treatment

Dept of Agriculture advises treating paddy seeds with Trichoderma viride (5g/kg seed) before nursery sowing to prevent soil-borne diseases. Procure seeds only from certified dealers.

Govt Support & Schemes

Krishak Bandhu

State Govt

Assured income up to Rs 10,000 per year per acre. In case of a farmer's untimely death (18-60 years), family receives a one-time grant of Rs 2 Lakh.

Apply/Details

PM-KISAN Samman Nidhi

Central Govt

Financial benefit of Rs 6,000 per year transferred directly into the bank accounts of farmer families across the country in three equal installments.

Apply/Details

Bangla Shasya Bima (BSB)

State Govt

100% premium paid by State Govt for crop insurance (except Sugarcane & Potato). Protects against yield loss due to natural calamities, pests, and diseases.

Apply/Details

Kisan Credit Card (KCC)

Central Govt

Adequate and timely credit support under a single window at highly subsidized interest rates for cultivation, machinery, and post-harvest expenses.
